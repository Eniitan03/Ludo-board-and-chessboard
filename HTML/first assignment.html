The success story of a village girl


This is a story of a stunning girl Shanti aged 16 years, lived in a backward area. She was very good in studies and also holds a ranking position at a government school and was very active in other co-curriculum activities too. She was a girl who was somehow very different from others. As she was of a village, people around her and her parents were of backward thinking.
